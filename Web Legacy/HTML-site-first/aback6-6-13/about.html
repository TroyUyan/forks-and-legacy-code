Welcome to the FalconDesign website. FalconDesign is a startup design firm located in Vancouver, WA. Started in 2013, the firm's goal is to provide affordable and professional graphics for businesses, sports teams, or personal branding. Since starting our services we have served over 50 clients locally, nationally, and internationally. We currently have three designers, whos individual porfolios can be viewed on our gallery page, and can be requested for your project.

We specialize in graphic design, providing affordable services for logo, banner, T-shirt, and print design! Digital graphics are sent to clients in locked-vector or internet graphic formats. T-shirts and other prints (i.e. buisness cards or bumper stickers) are outsourced for printing and then shipped to the client along with digital copies of the design. Every project is given a direct line of communication via a project-specific email. This allows our designers to send the client proofs and the client a chance to continue to feed the designer with ideas for the project.

FalconDesign is also on Facebook & Twitter! Keep an eye on these for our latest portfolio updates and specials! If you have any questions you can contact our official support email, with any design-specific questions before you order you can also ask our lead designer.
