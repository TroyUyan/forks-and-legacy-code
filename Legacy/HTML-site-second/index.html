What I Do

Hi, I’m Troy Uyan. It’d be hard to describe myself as anything but competition driven. I love sport and competition, and have been fairly successful in it. My non-academic or artistic history is quite interesting. It ranges from being a première-level soccer player, to being injured and becoming a semi-professional gamer and a four-time regional gaming champion.

Then to getting back on the pitch and playing semi-professional indoor soccer, then starting a career as a racing driver competing in local go-karting races and becoming a 14-time international sim racing drivers champion. What I Design

I started off this journey of design and development just doodling with Photoshop and creating graphics, logos and other image content for websites and communities I was involved with while I was in middle school. I turned that into a creative passion for design and art, but I wasn’t satisfied with just digital images.

I’ve ventured into print design, logo design, photography, and within the last year have used those to skills to beautify my introduction to web design and development. The long term goal is to turn this pallet of skills and knowledge of other platforms into a career as a web designer or front-end developer. Here’s to hoping I make it!
