Gaming Awards List

Welcome to my gaming Hall of Fame awards page!

This pages stands as my official "all-time list" for the greatest games I've ever played. They are divided into sections by genre or type, and ranked with a trophy system. A bronze trophy is arguably quite difficult to be awarded from me in the first place. But it signifies the game deserves recognition from the rest of the games of its type and made my Hall of Fame list. A silver trophy puts the game in a signature stature not to be overlooked. And finally the gold trophy signifies the game is one of the best games in the genre I have ever, or will ever play!
