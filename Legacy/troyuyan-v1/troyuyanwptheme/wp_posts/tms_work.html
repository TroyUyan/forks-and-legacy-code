Tatar Motorsports Work

Tatar Motorsports is the professional simulation racing team myself and my dad co-founded for our introductory season of competitive league racing. We've kept the team ever since, expanding it to a Senior and Raceworks team, winning over 30 team championships, and housing over 40 drivers over the years.

Links: Tatar Motorsports Team Page TMS Raceworks Team Page @TatarMotorsport on Twitter
